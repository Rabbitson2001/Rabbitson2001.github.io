What is this course about?

Digital media has become a staple in our daily lives, this course will equip candidates with the relevant digital skillset to pursue a career in this emerging discipline. Candidates will be trained to be competent across the digital media and marketing spectrum, including social media, user experience design, design thinking, online publishing, social advertising, content creation, and campaign planning.
